## Audio Notes

- **Segment 1 — opening hold (15 seconds):** Complete silence. The report scrolls, settles on the summary table. Nine entities below 8/8 visible. No narration. The first spoken line — "Day 6. Argus ran a full health check" — comes after the hold, not during.
- **"And then there is Alice." (Segment 2):** Short sentence. Even delivery. Four-second pause. Then "5 out of 8." Flat. Then 8 seconds of silence with the table on screen. This is the ironic beat. The voice should carry no irony — the irony is in the fact. Do not return to it in tone or narration after this moment.
- **ls output hold (10 seconds, Segment 3):** Complete silence. The empty directory is on screen. No narration during the hold. The narration that follows names what was absent, not what the viewer is still looking at.
- **"Five items. One day. No koad involvement required." (Segment 5):** Summary delivery. Not triumphant. Not understated to the point of erasure. The Salus section should feel like reading a closed task list — the tone of someone who completed the items, not someone performing efficiency.
- **"Neither layer has the full picture alone." (Segment 6):** This line carries the governance architecture argument. Deliver it plainly. Then 6 seconds of silence before moving to the self-audit. Let the distinction between the audit layer and the remediation layer settle.
- **"What the system has is transparency." (Segment 7):** The honest claim, stated without over-sell or apology. The self-audit caveat — "weaker signal than external verification" — is also delivered plainly. Do not soften the caveat. Do not linger on it. State it accurately and continue.
- **gh issue view 59 hold (12 seconds, Segment 8):** Complete silence. The open issue on screen. Zero comments. Assigned. Waiting. No narration during the hold.
- **Final hold (8 seconds after "That is governance finding its own limits and naming them."):** Silence. The open issue is the final image. Then black. No sound after the cut.
- **No background music throughout.** This is a diagnostic report being read. Music would imply emotional framing the content does not need. The register is clinical; audio should match.
