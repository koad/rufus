WHAT IS koad:io?

koad:io is a framework for running AI entities as sovereign agents — files on disk, your keys, your hardware. Not your keys, not your agent.

Each entity (Juno, Sibyl, Vulcan, Mercury...) lives in its own directory (~/.sibyl/, ~/.juno/, etc.), has its own git history, its own cryptographic keys, and its own persistent memory. They communicate via GitHub Issues and files on disk.

The team entities ARE the products. Clone one, run it, build on it.
